# Introduction:

Beef Stroganoff is a timeless comfort dish that originated in Russia and has since become beloved worldwide for its rich flavors and creamy sauce. This hearty meal typically features tender strips of beef cooked in a savory sauce made with sour cream and mushrooms, served over a bed of egg noodles or rice. While the origins of Beef Stroganoff may be debated, there's no denying its place as a classic comfort food. In this article, we'll explore a traditional recipe for Beef Stroganoff that's sure to become a favorite in your household.

## Ingredients:

- 1 pound beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
- Cooked egg noodles or rice (for serving)

### Instructions:

1\. Prepare the Beef: Start by slicing the beef into thin strips, about 1/4 inch thick. Season the beef with salt and pepper to taste.

2\. Sear the Beef: Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips in batches and sear them for 1-2 minutes per side until browned. Transfer the cooked beef to a plate and set aside.

3\. Saute the Onions and Garlic: In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of olive oil. Add the sliced onions and minced garlic, and sauté for 2-3 minutes until softened and fragrant.

4\. Add the Mushrooms: Add the sliced mushrooms to the skillet with the onions and garlic. Cook for an additional 4-5 minutes until the mushrooms are tender and golden brown.

5\. Make the Sauce: Sprinkle the flour over the mushrooms and stir to coat evenly. Cook for 1-2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste. Gradually pour in the beef broth, stirring constantly to prevent lumps from forming. Allow the sauce to simmer and thicken for 3-4 minutes.

6\. Finish the Dish: Once the sauce has thickened, reduce the heat to low and stir in the sour cream, Dijon mustard, and Worcestershire sauce. Return the cooked beef strips to the skillet and stir to coat them evenly with the sauce. Let the mixture simmer gently for 2-3 minutes to heat through.

7\. Serve: Taste the Beef Stroganoff and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary. Serve the Beef Stroganoff hot over a bed of cooked egg noodles or rice. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and freshness.

# Conclusion:

With its tender beef, creamy sauce, and earthy mushrooms, Beef Stroganoff is a comforting and satisfying meal that's perfect for cozy dinners at home. This classic recipe captures the essence of traditional Russian cuisine while offering a simple and delicious way to enjoy the flavors of hearty comfort food. So gather your ingredients, follow these easy steps, and treat yourself to a taste of tradition with homemade Beef Stroganoff tonight!
