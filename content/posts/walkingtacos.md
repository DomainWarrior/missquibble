# Introduction:

Walking tacos are a popular and convenient snack that combines the deliciousness of tacos with the portability of a handheld meal. Perfect for parties, picnics, or simply a fun dinner at home, homemade walking tacos are easy to make and customizable to suit your taste preferences. In this article, we'll walk you through the simple steps to create your own flavorful walking tacos that are sure to be a hit with family and friends.

## Ingredients:

- 1 pound ground beef or 1 pound ground chicken or ground turkey
- 1 packet taco seasoning mix
- 1 cup salsa
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 bag of your favorite tortilla chips (individual-sized bags work best)

Optional toppings:

- Diced onions
- Sliced jalapeños
- Black olives
- Guacamole
- Hot sauce
- Cilantro

### Instructions:

1\. Cook the Ground Beef: In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned and cooked through, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess grease from the skillet.

2\. Season the Beef: Add the taco seasoning mix to the cooked ground beef according to the package instructions. Stir well to combine and let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

3\. Prepare Toppings: While the beef is simmering, prepare your desired toppings. Chop the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, shred the cheese, and any other toppings you prefer.

4\. Assemble Walking Tacos: To assemble the walking tacos, simply open a bag of tortilla chips and crush them slightly to create space for the toppings. Then, spoon some of the seasoned ground beef into the bag on top of the crushed chips.

5\. Add Toppings: Next, layer on your favorite toppings such as salsa, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, and any other toppings you desire.

6\. Mix and Enjoy: Close the bag and gently shake or massage it to mix the ingredients together. Then, use a fork or spoon to eat the walking taco directly from the bag, or tear open the bag and use it as a makeshift bowl.

7\. Customize: Feel free to customize your walking tacos to suit your taste preferences. Add as much or as little of each topping as you like, and don't be afraid to get creative with different flavor combinations.

8\. Serve and Enjoy: Homemade walking tacos are best enjoyed immediately while the chips are still crunchy and the toppings are fresh. Serve them at your next gathering or enjoy them as a fun and easy dinner option.

#### Conclusion:

Homemade walking tacos are a delicious and fun twist on traditional tacos, perfect for parties, game days, or anytime you're craving a handheld meal on the go. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep work, you can create a customizable snack that's sure to please everyone. So grab your ingredients, assemble your walking tacos, and get ready to enjoy a flavorful and satisfying treat that's sure to become a new favorite!
