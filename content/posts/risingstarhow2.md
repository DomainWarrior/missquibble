### **Introduction**

# **Lets get back into it!** 

If you are new to Buskeria, we play as a busker, trying to work on our music carreer from various tasks like illegal busking on the streets of our hometown to open mic night at a local bar in our city. It is very important to do every low level mission at least 20 times. Before you consider moving on to the last mission try to aim for 100 missions of each low level.

On the top left of the page you will find your energy bar, if you are a much higher level your ego bar will also appear here.

The Card we start with is the busker. Then underneath we see the fan toatal, luck total, skill total and the multiplier from the cards.

under neath this is the number of cards in our collection.

The zone shows our map of buskeria the dark areas are what we have yet to unlock, the lighter areas are what we have unlocked. We will discuss the other buttons more as we progress in the video.

The diary is found at the bottom of the page, when streamers go live with rewards of rising star prizes (and hive/wax games alike) They are found here. You may battle in your first battle of the bands (BOB). The cards are used from ALL cards in the game, not the players collection. They are based on a points system that is then decided on what card will win the round. Players can find coffee to stop them from being taken out in a fight.

Diary is also a place that records your rewards from missions, the things you find ans well as when things become lost/empty such as clovers and coffee drinks.

Before we know it all those leveling grinds, we made some starbits AND now we are open to the local gig circuit. BUT WAIT

I keep running out of energy during low level grinding what is a good way to combat this? I recommend getting cold pizzas, they refresh your energy to 100% and refresh every 24 hours.

But I also notice I keep finding some coffees and discarded pizzas, is there any way to save these for later?

Why yes there is! you can buy a coffee mug as a well as a pizza box to keep a hold of these buffs for later.

Be aware, once you use it you cant use it again until you find more!

Oh But now I have a weird thing called EGO

What does this do?

so you reached level 15, you now have tons of fans and your ego is too big for your bridges, time to come down a little and become humble, you must grind music lessons now. Music lessons gives you skill points that will take the ego down. I recommend doing at least DOUBLE the amount of fans you currently have. As you do missions you regain drunk fans and they boost your ego debuff. So to combat that if you have double the skills then you do fans you should be good for a while without having to worry about that buff.

Now Before you know it you are level 40!

Congrats on making it this far! Now its time to focus on grinding for starbits, when you collect 10k you can buy more pizzas at 100k you can get card packs and at 1Million you became a Millionaire! You get a super rare card that is non-transferrable/saleable.
